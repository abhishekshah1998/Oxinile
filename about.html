About Page

Jyotsan Foods and Beverages

Jyotsan Foods & Bevereges, formerly Shete Foods and Bevereges is a company formed by the efforts of Mr. Shete in 2010 with a goal to provide hyegeinc foods and bevereges to each and every house. The firm was re-established as Jyotsan Foods & Bevereges in the year 2015 by a group of young aspirants who wish to see a brighter tomorrow where the people "DRINK SMARTER" by restarting the brand OXINILE. Since then, OXINILE has been established as one of the major brands in the Pune city and its outskirts.
